4TH SEMISTER SOCIAL MEDIA MARKETING

UNIT I: Social Media Marketing

  • Building an online community: This topic focuses on the importance of building an online community through social media platforms. It covers strategies for engaging with the audience, creating valuable content, and fostering a sense of community.
  • Understanding Social Media Marketing: This topic provides an overview of social media marketing, its significance in today's digital landscape, and the benefits it offers to businesses. It explores different social media platforms and their unique features.
  • Marketing and building presence on Facebook: This topic delves into the process of marketing on Facebook. It covers creating a Facebook business page, optimizing the page for engagement, utilizing Facebook's advertising options, and best practices for promoting products or services on the platform.
  • Marketing and building presence on Twitter: This topic focuses on leveraging Twitter as a marketing tool. It covers creating an effective Twitter profile, engaging with followers, using hashtags, and implementing Twitter advertising strategies.
  • Employer branding on LinkedIn: This topic explores the concept of employer branding and its application on LinkedIn. It covers creating a compelling LinkedIn company page, showcasing company culture and values, and leveraging LinkedIn's features for recruitment and talent acquisition.
  • Facebook advertising overview: This topic provides an overview of Facebook advertising, including its targeting capabilities, ad formats, and campaign objectives. It explains how Facebook ads work and the potential benefits for businesses.
  • How to create Facebook ads: This topic delves into the process of creating Facebook ads. It covers ad design, ad targeting options, budgeting, and bidding strategies. It also discusses ad optimization and measuring campaign performance.
  • Additional advertising options and best practices for Facebook advertising: This topic explores additional advertising options available on Facebook, such as carousel ads, lead generation ads, and video ads. It also covers best practices for optimizing ad performance, including ad testing, tracking conversions, and campaign optimization techniques.
  • Marketing and monetizing on YouTube: This topic focuses on leveraging YouTube for marketing and monetization purposes. It covers creating a YouTube channel, optimizing video content, engaging with the audience, and exploring YouTube's monetization options.
  • Customize your YouTube Channel: This topic explains how to customize a YouTube channel to align with a brand's identity. It covers channel art, thumbnails, playlists, and organizing content effectively.
  • Video optimization on YouTube: This topic explores strategies for optimizing videos on YouTube to improve visibility and reach. It covers keyword research, video titles, descriptions, tags, and thumbnail optimization.
  • YouTube Analytics: This topic introduces YouTube Analytics and its importance in measuring video performance. It covers key metrics, audience insights, and using data to refine video marketing strategies.

UNIT II: Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and Online Advertising

  • Understanding SEO: This topic provides an introduction to search engine optimization. It covers the importance of SEO for website visibility, organic traffic, and user experience. It explains the fundamental concepts and principles of SEO.
  • Content optimization: This topic focuses on optimizing website content for search engines. It covers keyword research, on-page optimization techniques, meta tags, headings, image optimization, and content quality guidelines.
  • Long-term content planning: This topic emphasizes the significance of long-term content planning for SEO. It covers content strategy, content calendars, topic research, and aligning content with target audience needs and search intent.
  • Keyword strategy: This topic explores the process of developing an effective keyword strategy for SEO. It covers keyword research tools, competitive analysis, long-tail keywords, and optimizing content around targeted keywords.
  • Link building strategies: This topic delves into link building strategies to improve website authority and search engine rankings. It covers acquiring high-quality backlinks, link outreach, guest blogging, and link earning techniques.
  • Measuring SEO effectiveness: This topic focuses on measuring the effectiveness of SEO efforts. It covers key performance indicators (KPIs), SEO analytics tools, tracking organic traffic, keyword rankings, and evaluating SEO ROI.
  • SEO for Ecommerce: This topic discusses SEO strategies specific to ecommerce websites. It covers product optimization, category pages, site structure, user experience, and optimizing for mobile devices.
  • Local search: This topic explores local SEO strategies for businesses targeting a specific geographic area. It covers local keyword targeting, Google My Business optimization, online reviews, and local citation building.
  • Mobile SEO: This topic emphasizes the importance of mobile optimization for SEO. It covers mobile-friendly design, responsive web design, page speed optimization, and mobile-specific ranking factors.
  • Pay-Per-Click Advertising: This topic introduces the concept of pay-per-click (PPC) advertising. It covers the basics of PPC, its benefits, and different PPC platforms. It focuses on getting started with Google AdWords.
  • Advertising tracking: This topic explores the tracking and measurement of online advertising campaigns. It covers conversion tracking, setting up tracking pixels, Google Analytics integration, and campaign performance analysis.
  • Key Google AdWords strategies: This topic covers key strategies and best practices for running successful Google AdWords campaigns. It includes keyword research, ad copywriting, bidding strategies, ad extensions, and campaign optimization techniques.
  • Remarketing with Google: This topic explains remarketing and its application using Google AdWords. It covers creating remarketing lists, setting up remarketing campaigns, and best practices for effective remarketing.
  • Budget and ROI tips: This topic focuses on budget management and maximizing return on investment (ROI) in online advertising campaigns. It covers budget allocation, bid optimization, cost-per-acquisition (CPA) management, and tracking ROI.
  • B2B Remarketing Campaigns: This topic explores remarketing strategies specifically for B2B (business-to-business) marketing. It covers targeting decision-makers, creating custom audiences, and remarketing tactics for B2B lead generation.
