Prose and Poetry:

  • "Man Is the Architect of His Own Future" by Gurrapu Damodar: This prose piece explores the theme of personal agency and the power of human actions in shaping one's destiny.
  • "Mehta Gullie" by Ved Prakash Mehta: This prose work delves into the life and experiences of a young boy named Mehta Gullie, offering insights into his worldview and struggles.
  • "P.V Narasimha Rao: The Literateur" by Palakurthy Dhinakar: This piece highlights the literary contributions and achievements of P.V Narasimha Rao, an eminent figure in Indian politics.

GRAMMAR TOPICS

  • Seen Passages: This section involves reading and comprehending passages provided in the question paper. Students are required to answer questions based on their understanding of the text.
  • Formal Letter Writing: This topic focuses on the principles and conventions of writing formal letters. Students learn the format, language, and appropriate tone for different types of formal letters.
  • Communication Skills: This section emphasizes the development of effective communication skills, including verbal and non-verbal communication, listening skills, and interpersonal communication.
  • Report Writing: Students learn the format and structure of writing reports, including gathering information, organizing data, and presenting findings in a clear and concise manner.
  • Meeting Minutes (MOM): This topic covers the skills required for taking accurate and comprehensive meeting minutes. Students learn how to record important points, decisions, and actions during meetings.
  • Unseen Passages: Similar to seen passages, this section involves reading and comprehending passages that students have not previously encountered. Questions are based on the understanding of the text.
  • Debate: Students learn the art of debating, including argumentation, logical reasoning, and effective presentation skills. They engage in structured debates on given topics, presenting their viewpoints and counterarguments.
  • Group Discussion: This topic focuses on developing skills for effective group discussions. Students learn to express their opinions, actively listen, and collaborate with others to arrive at a consensus.
  • Speech: Students learn the techniques of delivering speeches, including structuring the content, using appropriate language and tone, and engaging the audience effectively.
  • Vote of Thanks: This topic covers the art of delivering a vote of thanks, expressing gratitude, and acknowledging individuals or organizations for their contributions or support.
